How did i end up in Portugal?:
My parents were both born in Angola ( Mother(Benguela) - Father ( Luanda). They lived their early childhood in Angola until they were forced to run away due to the civil war. They both moved to POrtugal ( where they would meet and start dating), after a few years theydecided to move to South Africa wheremy brother and I would later be born. After living all our lives in Africa my parents felt like the best decision for my future would be to move, and simce they had a prior life, as well as family in Portugal, it seemed like the best option.

Educational Background: While living in South Africa ; I had a great love for biology, as well as architecture and electrical engineering ( My love for Engineering came from my father who started his own, high voltage electrical company). However, my lack of motivation in school meant that I would not focus suffieciently in my mathematic classes , which is the main part of having a engineering course.
When I moved to Portugal , my father suggested that I follow a business marketing and manegment course, which I thought would be a good idea.

My Business course: This idea was short lived, I applied at a university called Universidade Atlantica, who assured I would have classes done in English. On the day that the course began I told that there were not enough Internaational students to create a seperate english course. What was next?
Since I had to leave the previous university , it was too late to find a new univesity as the courses were no longer acceptingnew students.
