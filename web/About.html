What is IADE Reporter ?


Our Websites goal is to achieve easier communication of infrastructural problems within the IADE Santos Campus.
This site is to be used as a tool for all, studens, and visitors alike , to help raise awarness of whats going on in our beloved campus.

Are you a student?


We know that as a student, you either dont have the time, or enegry
to walk to student services to make a complain about a broken fixture.
But , with this site , the time and energy required is little -to non.
Help us by reporting anything you feel needs to be improved within our university so that together we can grow and build a greater campus.
Our future starts with you.
